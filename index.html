Empowering the Next Generation

ShiftQuest Africa is committed to empowering young people in grassroots communities through leadership development, purpose, innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship. We create platforms—such as youth summits and mentorship forums—to equip young people with the skills, networks, and mindset needed to thrive in a changing world and develop solutions to challenges within their communities.

Our Programs

Youth Summit

Our annual flagship innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership gathering featuring panels, workshops, and networking.

PowerTalk

High-impact mentorship and knowledge sharing through short, powerful talks by industry professionals and successful youth leaders.

Youth Circuits

A mobile community engagement initiative rotating across wards to bring vital workshops directly to grassroots areas.

Community Outreach

Social impact and service projects involving children's home visits, environmental conservation, and school mentorship.

Elevate Program

A structured mentorship program connecting youth with experienced professionals through intimate small group circles and coaching.
